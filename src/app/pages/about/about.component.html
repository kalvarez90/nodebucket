Welcome to Nodebucket!


Hi! And welcome to our new application, Nodebucket, where you will be able to keep all your tasks in one single place. The purpose and goal of this application is to help you track your daily goals in a simple and easy way. You will be able to add as many tasks as you need to keep track of any goals you have for your day, month, or year… Is all up to you.

Nodebucket is part of a four week project create for our last course at Bellevue University, Web-450. This project consists of a task management software that has been designed to help simplify the way you create, track, edit, and delete time sensitive material. It can help you keep track of your daily tasks by adding, editing, and deleting your to-do and done tasks. Nodebucket has been created with the MEAN Stack technologies; MongoDB, Express, Angular, ad Node.js. We also used SoapUI to create APIs testing and make sure certain IDs worked as intended. When using this application, you can move your items from the to do list to the done list by simple grabbing and dragging the item to the designated area. You can also arrange these depending on importance, or relevance. Once no longer needed, you can remove from your list by simply clicking on the three dots at the right of each item and selecting “remove.” This powerful yet simple task management software gives you the best tools when it comes to creating, organizing, assigning, tracking, and deleting tasks.
